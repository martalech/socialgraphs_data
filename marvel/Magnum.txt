They are Hydron, lord of the waters; Magnum, master of the earth; Hellfire, wielder of flame; and Zephyr, mistress of the winds. The Elementals used [[N'Kantu, the Living Mummy]] as a pawn against the [[Living Monolith]] to obtain the Ruby Scarab from them. However, Zephyr betrayed the other Elementals and allied with N'Kantu. The Elementals attacked Zephyr, the Living Mummy, and their allies and gained the Scarab from them. When the Elementals tried to release their energies through the Scarab, they were blasted off Earth.<ref>''Supernatural Thriller'' #9\u201315. Marvel Comics.</ref>\n\nThe Elementals were later returned to Earth and pursued Zephyr and the Scarab, coming into conflict with the entity [[Hecate (Marvel Comics)|Hecate]]. Taking Zephyr hostage, Hellfire and Hydron forced her allies to recover the Scarab.
