The Resistants' protest against the United States' [[Registration Acts (comics)#Mutant Registration Act|Mutant Registration Act]] was cut short by a clash with [[U.S. Agent (comics)|John Walker]], who was the current [[Captain America]].<ref>''Captain America'' #343</ref><ref>''Captain America'' #346</ref> Later, the Resistants revert to their Mutant Force identities and costumes.<ref>''Captain America'' #426</ref> Their next clash is with the [[New Warriors]].<ref>''The New Warriors'' vol.2 #6</ref>\n\nParalyzer later battles [[Spider-Man]], subduing him with a lucky blow. Spider-Man later teams up with several members of the [[Midnight Sons]] who were investigating the activities of a more demonic [[Doppelganger (comics)|Spidey-doppelganger]]. While pursuing this creature, the heroes discover and stop Paralyzer's plan to recreate [[Zzzax]].<ref>''Midnight Sons Umlimited'' #3 (Oct. 1, 1993)</ref>\n\nAfter the [[Sentinel (comics)|Sentinels]] destroy [[Genosha]], Paralyzer, again called the '''Shocker''', helps [[Toad (Marvel Comics)|Toad]] and other mutants create a statue of Genosha's former ruler Magneto. Their group was not sure if they would take Unus back if, in fact, he had survived.
